05 / About

A real shop. Not a feed.

LocationUptown Oxford, OH
ShopuhpBrick-and-mortar
Hours7 days / week
WeBuy · Sell · Trade

Flow State Vintage started with a simple idea: Oxford deserves a vintage shop that takes the rack as seriously as a gallery does the wall. Curated, not cluttered.

We travel for the good stuff — estate sales, old gyms, road trips that end in a U-Haul — and bring it back to 37 W High. Every piece gets a once-over before it hits the floor. If it doesn't move us, it doesn't hang.

Come in between classes. Bring a friend. Bring the denim you never wear. Flip through slow. That's the whole idea.
